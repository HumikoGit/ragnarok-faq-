Ragnarok: Sharing Hero — FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the game, blockchain, and rewards.

🎮 Basic Game Information
What is this game?
Ragnarok: Sharing Hero is a blockchain RPG based on the renowned Ragnarok IP. A large-scale faction war game where Cosmos and Chaos factions compete in real-time for control of Midgard. Developed by Funigloo, a global studio with 17 years of experience (creators of Dragonica Mobile, Seal Mobile, and LaTale W).
When was the game released?
Officially launched on March 26, 2026.
Where can I download it?
Available free on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
— Google Play: Search "Ragnarok Sharing Hero"
— App Store: Search "Ragnarok Sharing Hero"
Is it free or paid?
Basic download and play is free. In-game item purchases and NFT trading may require additional costs.
⚔️ Gameplay
How do you play?
Develop your Knight, strengthen equipment, hunt bosses, and battle other players in real-time on the Middle Land battlefield. Character growth through Crafting, Evolution, and Enhancement systems. Borrow other players' characters for Demon Lord Raids.
What is PvP?
PvP (Player vs Player) is direct combat between players. Valhalla and Helheim factions clash in real-time on Middle Land. The controlling faction grants all members 2x gathering reward buff and stat increase buff.
What are Cosmos and Chaos?
The two factions in the game. Every player belongs to Cosmos (order) or Chaos. Both factions compete for control of Midgard in real-time. If one faction becomes too strong, the system automatically activates character creation limits to restore balance.
How do I get Heroes?
Develop a Knight directly in-game, or borrow heroes via the Character Sharing system. Borrowing powerful heroes makes Demon Lord Raids, Kingdom Quests, and Knight Order content easier.
What is Demon Lord Raid?
Main enemies players must defeat together. Hunt Demon Lords and final boss Woodunkeu to obtain "Mint Power".
What is the Ranking System?
Participate in 10+ ranking types to earn rewards. Weekly Mint Grand Prix (NFT, MVP Kill, Share Ranking) based on Mint Power, plus PvP, World Boss, Season, and Class rankings.
What is the Sharing System?
Borrow a friend's character for your Knight Order to tackle harder content together. The lending player earns sharing rewards.
🔗 Blockchain / NFT
What is Immutable?
Specialized blockchain gaming platform. A global company based in Australia, founded in 2018, enabling true ownership of in-game items as NFTs.
— Immutable Network: Blockchain for game assets
— Immutable Passport: Login & integrated wallet (6M+ users)
— Immutable Play: Quest, reward & leaderboard platform
Are there NFT items?
Yes. Mint in-game equipment as NFT "Digital Trophies" that permanently record your achievements. Rare items can be displayed in the NFT Gallery to buff other players.
Where can I buy NFT items?
At the TokenTrove marketplace. Freely buy and sell NFT equipment obtained or crafted in-game.
How do I get $MINT tokens?
$MINT is earned through in-game activities, not purchased directly.
— Raid: Hunt boss Woodunkeu for "Mint Power"
— Character/Equipment Sharing: Earn $MINT by lending
— Complete Immutable Play Quests for $MINT airdrops
Total Reward Pool: $70,000 — $10K Launch Airdrop + $60K Weekly
Do I need a wallet?
Logging into Immutable Passport with Google/Apple/Facebook auto-creates a wallet. You can play without a separate wallet if you just want to enjoy the game.
