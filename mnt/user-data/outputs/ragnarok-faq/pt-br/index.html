🎮 Basic Game Information
Q. What is this game?
Ragnarok: Sharing Hero is a blockchain RPG based on the renowned Ragnarok IP. It is a large-scale faction war game where the Cosmos and Chaos factions compete in real-time to control Midgard. Developed by Funigloo, a global game studio with 17 years of experience (creators of Dragonica Mobile, Seal Mobile, and LaTale W).
Q. When was the game released?
Officially launched on March 26, 2026.
Q. Where can I download it?
Available for free on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
- Google Play: Search 'Ragnarok Sharing Hero'
- App Store: Search 'Ragnarok Sharing Hero'
Q. Is it free or paid?
Basic download and play is free. In-game item purchases and NFT trading may require additional costs.
⚔️ Gameplay
Q. How do you play?
Develop your Knight, strengthen equipment, hunt bosses, and battle other players in real-time on the Middle Land battlefield.
- Character growth through Crafting, Evolution, and Enhancement systems
- Borrow other players' characters to join Demon Lord Raids
- Various content including Raids, Kingdom Quests, World Bosses, and PvP
Q. What is PvP?
PvP (Player vs Player) is content where players battle directly against each other. The Valhalla faction and Helheim faction clash in real-time on the Middle Land battlefield. Battle results determine territorial control and rewards per faction. The faction controlling Middle Land grants all members 2x gathering reward buff and stat increase buff.
Q. What are Cosmos and Chaos?
The two factions in the game. Every player belongs to either Cosmos (order) or Chaos. Both factions compete for control of Midgard in real-time. If one faction becomes too powerful, the system automatically activates character creation restrictions to restore balance.
Q. How do I get Heroes?
You can develop a Knight directly in the game, or borrow heroes from other players through the Character Sharing system. Players who borrow powerful heroes can more easily progress through Demon Lord Raids, Kingdom Quests, and Knight Order buff content.
Q. What is Demon Lord Raid?
The main enemies in the game that players must defeat together. By hunting Demon Lords at each level and the final boss Woodunkeu, you can obtain 'Mint Power'.
Q. What is the Ranking System?
Players can strategically participate in more than 10 types of rankings to earn rewards. Including the weekly Mint Grand Prix (NFT, MVP Kill, Share Ranking) based on Mint Power rewards, as well as PvP, World Boss, Season, and Class ranking reward systems.
Q. What is the Sharing System?
Borrow a friend's character and add it to your Knight Order to play content together. This allows you to challenge harder content, and the friend who lent the character receives sharing rewards.
🔗 Blockchain / NFT
Q. What is Immutable?
Immutable is a specialized blockchain gaming platform. It is a global company based in Australia, founded in 2018, that allows players to truly own in-game items as digital assets (NFTs).
- Immutable Network: Blockchain network for game assets
- Immutable Passport: Game login and integrated wallet solution (over 6 million registered users)
- Immutable Play: Quest, reward, and leaderboard platform
Q. Are there NFT items?
Yes. You can mint in-game equipment as NFTs. NFT equipment is a 'Digital Trophy' that permanently records player achievements and journeys. Rare items can be displayed in the NFT Gallery to provide buffs to other players.
Q. Where can I buy NFT items?
Ragnarok: Sharing Hero NFT equipment can be purchased at the TokenTrove marketplace. Players can freely buy and sell NFT equipment obtained or crafted in-game.
